Gem Collector

You will be given a Goal Number at the start of the game.

There are four gems below. By clicking on a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

If you match the Goal Number - YOU WIN!

If you go over the Goal Number - YOU LOSE!

Pro tip: Each time you start a new game, the value of the gems will be different and the Goal Number will change!

Good Luck!
